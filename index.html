About Me

Growing up I spent a lot of my time playing chat based roleplaying games where it was necessary to use HTML and CSS to add images and colored text to enhance the gaming experience. I have since earned a Bachelor's Degree of Science in Information Technology at Western Governors University. During my degree program I had a couple of Web Development courses that rekindled my love for coding. I have since enrolled in VSchool, which is a Web Development Bootcamp, to help me gain the skills that I didn't get from degree. My ultimate goal is to become an SME in my industry and I plan to accomplish that by continuing my education through classes or personal experience.
